> This page preserves the structure of the former Critiques section. It does not evaluate a real paper or technical paradigm.

## Claim

State the subject's central claim accurately before evaluating it.

## Analysis

Inspect gaps between the method, assumptions, evidence, and conclusion.

## Evidence / Counterexamples

Record verifiable data, citations, reproductions, or counterexamples.

## Verdict

Give a provisional judgment proportional to the evidence and state its limitations.
